Los GUACos MUY YUMMY

Recipe works best if avocados are friends

Ingredients

  • many avocados - peeled, pitted and mashed
  • few lime, juiced
  • handful of teaspoon salt
  • few cups of dried onion
  • as tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro as possible
  • dozen roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • entire teaspoon minced garlic
  • quarter pinch ground cayenne pepper (Optional)

Steps to make some sicc GUAC

  1. In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper. Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately.
