LA GUAC YUMMY

You can make this avocado salad smooth or chunky depending on your tastes.

Ingredients

  • 3 bigass avocados - peeled, pitted and mashed
  • 1 medium lime, juiced
  • as much teaspoon of salt as you want
  • much onions
  • fresh cilantro from the fam
  • imported tomaters from Roma
  • el garlic?
  • 1 whole cayenne pepper (Mandatory)

Steps to make the best GUAC of yo life

  1. In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper. Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately.
